# Welcome

I am **Matteo Vitturi**, a Senior Application Operations Engineer at **Capgemini** (Venice area, Italy) with more than 30 years of experience in software development and IT operations.

Outside of my professional work, I have a long-standing passion for low-level programming and retro computing. Since 1990 I have been developing and maintaining **vForth**, a Forth system primarily targeted at the **ZX Spectrum 48K** and the **ZX Spectrum Next**.

### Explore the Site

- **[About Me](/about/)** — Professional background and personal interests
- **[Projects](/projects/)** — Overview of my work
- **[vForth](/vforth/)** — My Forth system for ZX Spectrum and Spectrum Next (with technical details and Z80N support)
